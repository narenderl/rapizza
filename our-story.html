our story

Multicultural Vibes

Canada is multicultural—a blend of many cultures from around the world. This is the real source of our social harmony and our success as an industrial economy of Canada. Rapizza represents the same multicultural vibes with one the most diverse menu in Canada, serving both the western mainstream and eastern exotic recipes.

Fusion of East & West

And on top of that, we've got something fresh—a fusion of those eastern and western recipes. Our fusion pizza range blends the currysome flavors from the Indian subcontinent and the art of Italian pizza baking. So, are you a tastemaker? Come, experience the exotic fusion pizzas, such as Tandoori Shrimp Pizza, Malabar coast Pizza, Chicken Angara Pizza.

Something for Everyone

Are you coming in with your family? We've got the whole menu straight from India, such as Butter Chicken, Malai Tikka, Shahi Paneer, Chicken Tikka, for you and a whole bunch of desserts for kids. Organizing a corporate event? Order from a wide range of sandwiches, pizzas, salads, and desserts.
